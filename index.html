Frequently asked questions

What is Mateba?

Mateba creates personalised songs from real stories, memories, relationships, and life events.

Is this AI-generated music?

Mateba uses AI-assisted music tools as part of the production process, but the emotional direction, story interpretation, lyric shaping, and final guidance are handled personally.

How personal can the song be?

The song can include names, places, memories, phrases, family details, and emotional references that matter to the people involved.

How do I start?

The simplest way is to send a WhatsApp message. We will guide you through the first questions.

Can I choose the genre?

Yes. You can suggest a genre, mood, tempo, vocal feel, or reference direction. Mateba will help shape it into something that fits the story.

Can I request changes?

Yes. The process includes space for thoughtful refinements.

How long does it take?

Timing depends on the story, the occasion, and the level of refinement required. If there is a deadline, share it in the first WhatsApp message.

Can this be used for weddings or memorials?

Yes. Mateba songs can be created for weddings, anniversaries, birthdays, memorials, family tributes, and other meaningful moments.

What if the story is sensitive?

Sensitive stories are handled privately and respectfully. Only share what you are comfortable sharing.

Who owns the final song?

Usage terms should be confirmed during the order process. Mateba will explain what you may do with the final song before production begins.
