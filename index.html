What is Mateba?

Mateba is a custom music service. You share the story behind a person, relationship or life event. We turn that story into a personalised song designed to feel specific, honest and meaningful.

When people use it

Milestone birthdays

A song built from family memories, inside jokes, life lessons and the person’s journey.

Anniversaries

A personal way to tell the story of a relationship, from where it started to what it means now.

Memorials

A respectful song that honours a life, preserves memories and gives family something lasting.

Special gifts

For people who already have things, but would value something personal and impossible to buy off a shelf.

How it works

1. Start a conversation

You contact us on WhatsApp and tell us who the song is for.

2. Share the story

We guide you through the details: memories, people, moments, tone and purpose.

3. We create the song

The story is shaped into lyrics, structure, style and music.

4. You receive it

You receive a finished personalised song that can be shared, gifted or kept.

Example use case

A 50th birthday song for a father

His children share memories about his work ethic, humour, sacrifices, favourite sayings and the way he shaped their lives. Mateba turns those details into a personalised song that feels like it could only belong to him.
