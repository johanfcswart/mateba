What is an Audiograph?

A photograph captures how someone looked. An Audiograph captures who they are.

An Audiograph is a deeply personal musical work shaped around a real person, relationship, life event, memory, or story. It is not merely a custom song. It is an emotional record: a way to preserve connection, identity, meaning, and memory through music.

Legal and sensitivity note

Mateba may decline or request changes to projects involving legal disputes, confidential information, third-party privacy, minors, medical trauma, bereavement, family conflict, or other sensitive matters. Where relevant, clients are responsible for ensuring they have the right to share source material, names, recordings, photographs, and personal information used in the project.

Mateba creates expressive musical works. We do not provide legal, psychological, medical, or counselling services.

FAQ

Is Mateba a custom song service?

No. Mateba creates Audiographs. Music is the medium, but the core value is emotional preservation, expression, and replayable connection.

What can an Audiograph be created for?

Weddings, anniversaries, new life, coming of age, tributes, memorials, family legacy, milestones, and deeply personal gifts.

Do I need to know exactly what I want?

No. You need a person, story, relationship, or moment. Mateba helps structure the emotional material before production.

Can Mateba work with sensitive stories?

Sometimes. Sensitive matters may require additional care, boundaries, permissions, or refusal where the project is not appropriate.

How do I start?

Use the Contact Mateba button and share the basic context. Mateba will respond with the next step.
