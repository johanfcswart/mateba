What Mateba is

Mateba is a custom music service for people who want a gift with emotional weight. You share the story behind a person, relationship or life event. We shape those details into a complete song that feels specific, honest and personal.

The value is not only the music. It is the recognition: names, memories, sacrifices, humour, places and moments that make the song unmistakably about that person.

When people use it

Mateba is designed for moments where a standard gift feels too small, too generic or too easy to forget.

Milestone birthdays

A song built from family memories, inside jokes, life lessons and the person’s journey.

Anniversaries

A personal way to tell the story of a relationship, from where it started to what it means now.

Memorials

A respectful song that honours a life, preserves memories and gives family something lasting.

Special gifts

For people who already have things, but would value something personal and impossible to buy off a shelf.
