What is an Audiograph?

A photograph captures how someone looked. An Audiograph captures who they are.

An Audiograph is a deeply personal musical work: a musical portrait shaped around real moments and real people. It preserves emotional identity, connection, and meaning through music.

Sensitivity and legal note

Mateba may decline or request changes to projects involving legal disputes, confidential information, third-party privacy, minors, medical trauma, bereavement, family conflict, or other sensitive matters. Clients are responsible for ensuring they may share the names, stories, recordings, images, and personal information provided.

Mateba creates expressive musical works. Mateba does not provide legal, medical, psychological, or counselling services.

FAQ

Is Mateba just a custom song service?

No. Mateba creates Audiographs. Music is the medium; emotional preservation is the value.

What can an Audiograph be for?

Weddings, anniversaries, new life, coming of age, tributes, memorials, family legacy, and meaningful milestones.

Do I need to know exactly what I want?

No. You need a person, relationship, story, or moment. Mateba helps shape the brief.

Can Mateba work with sensitive stories?

Sometimes. Sensitive stories require care, consent, discretion, and clear boundaries.

Can Mateba imitate someone’s voice?

No unauthorised voice or likeness imitation is allowed. Mateba will not knowingly imitate a real person, artist, public figure, deceased person, or private individual without appropriate permission.

Can I share the final Audiograph online?

Possibly, depending on the agreed usage rights and whether the Audiograph contains private or sensitive material involving others.
